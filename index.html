The Ridge

Screenshots

The Ridge is a Left 4 Dead inspired multiplayer FPS. Set in an underwater base, you are the only survivor after a failed experiment and now the base is filled with infected. Your mission is to escape

    My Contributions

  • PhysX Ragdolls.
    • Using Nvidia PhysX to simulate ragdolls. The ragdolls were exported from Autodesk Maya using Nvidia PhysX DCC Plugin and then imported into our engine.
  • Inventory system and HUD.
    • Health bar on players, ammo count hud, using items and quick swap item using keyboard.
  • Weapons and items.
    • All interactable items like heal, weapons and the ability to give item to other players.
  • Crosshair.
    • A crosshair system for different weapon types.
  • Pickups.
    • Picking up different items within range that can be found across the map and add it to the inventory.

    Project Details

  • 9 weeks half time.
  • Achieved with our own engine.
  • C++ extended by Lua scripting.
  • JSON and custom data formats.
  • APIs: DirectX 11, Wwise, FFmpeg

Candy Crush Friends Saga

Screenshots

Candy Crush Friends Saga 2018

Your Candy Crush Friends have been scattered throughout the Candy Kingdom - Join this match 3 puzzle game to find them! This friendly bunch have their own unique powers to help you to create sweet candy combinations and blast through blockers!

My Contributions

  • Candy Surprise.
    • Reworked the experience of opening a candy surprise from flow, visuals, and interactions. - Candy Surprise is the main driver for our collection system. It's a box that contains stickers that you need to unlock charcters and boosters.
  • Live Ops.
    • Worked on creating and organizing Bubblegum troll event which was running through the summer of 2019. The event was a team event and was active for only a limited amount of time and the reward was the Bubblegum troll character. The players that played even further after unlocking the character also got a skin for him.
  • Quest System.
    • Parsing for reading and saving quests and introduction tutorial.
  • Pre Game Screen.
    • Reworked the pre game screen with better visuals and to showcase all the available characters for the level and the ability to hotswap between their respective outfits.

  • Date: August 2017 to September 2019
  • Studio: King
